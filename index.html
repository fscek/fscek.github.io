about

(definitely not written by someone else)

SZCH (pronounced [ʃt͡ɕ]) is a DJ and producer hailing from the Istrian peninsula and currently based in Zagreb, Croatia.

Belonging to the new generation of Croatian artists, SZCH's productions and sets are encapsulated in his characteristic meme and online microculture influenced humour.


When paired with his high energy, that allows him to cross traditional genre boundaries in club music and beyond, using various edits, unanticipated BPM switches and distinctive mixing techniques as set pieces; be it UK hardcore, baile-Jersey club fusions, gqom or Japanese garage-flavoured hyperpop.

As an autodidactic artist, he is unrestrained by conventional artistic influences and manages to set himself apart while still capturing and mastering the essence of his expressiveness and carrying it through his unique style.


He has introduced his music across the European continent, including Germany, France, Ukraine, the Netherlands, Switzerland, the UK, and Poland. Alongside his performances, he has also made notable appearances in live radio shows at Red Light Radio, Operator, Berlin Community Radio, Balamii, and through recorded shows for NTS Radio and LYL.

His work has caught the attention of notable artists like Objekt, rRoxymore, Ross From Friends, Peggy Gou, Baltra, and Charlie Bones, among others.


Most recently, Value, initiated by SZCH, is a project rooted in Zagreb with a global reach, focusing on the club community. It serves as a platform that bridges the online and physical spaces, spotlighting talents in music, art, and photography.

The essence of Value lies in its commitment to providing a space for creative expression and connection, through club nights, live performances, and exhibitions. It's a place where both new and established artists can share their work in an environment that values subtlety, intellectual engagement, and community.
